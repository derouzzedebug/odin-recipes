Steak

My favorite way to cook a perfect steak!

Ingredients

For the steak:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 basil sprig
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Montreal steak seasoning

Instructions

Prepare Steak

  1. Preheat your skillet to high heat.
  2. Allow the garlic, onion and basil to sizzle for a minute or two.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  4. Brush the steaks with olive oil and season generously with salt, black pepper, and Montreal steak seasoning on both sides.
  5. Place the steaks on the grill or skillet and cook for about 2-3minutes per side for medium-rare.
  6. Place skillet in the oven and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the desired level of doneness is reached.
  7. Remove the steaks from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
